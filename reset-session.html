How to Reset a Browser Session

If your browser session is stuck, expired, or behaving incorrectly, a quick session reset can usually fix the problem. This does not delete saved passwords or bookmarks.

Quick steps

  1. Close all browser tabs
  2. Open a new private or incognito window
  3. Reload the affected website
  4. If the issue persists, restart the browser

Most session-related issues are temporary and resolve automatically after a refresh or restart.
